Welcome!

Welcome to my homepage! I am a mathematician with research interests in optimal transport and its applications to metric geometry, mathematical relativity, and PDEs.

Currently, I am a Bernoulli Instructor @ EPFL.

Before, I held a Postdoctoral Fellowship in the research group of Professor Robert McCann @ Department of Mathematics, University of Toronto. In between, I was a Fields Postdoctoral Fellow @ Fields Institute for Research in Mathematical Sciences, supervised by Professor Robert Haslhofer, Professor Vitali Kapovitch, Professor Robert McCann, and Professor Adrian Nachman. I was associated with the Thematic Program on Nonsmooth Riemannian and Lorentzian Geometry.

I completed my doctoral studies supervised by Professor Karl-Theodor Sturm @ Institute for Applied Mathematics, University of Bonn.

If you have any questions, please feel free to contact me!
